* Smooth button interactions
* Icon micro-animations

* Card hover shadow expansion
* Elegant loading states
* Smooth modal transitions
* Animated background layers

* Subtle parallax where appropriate

Motion must feel:

* Expensive
* Intelligent

* High-end
* Smooth
